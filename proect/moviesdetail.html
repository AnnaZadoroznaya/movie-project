Описание:
What if one day you are possessed by a symbiote that gives you superhuman powers? But Venom is a completely unkind symbiote, and it is impossible to come to an agreement with him. Is it necessary to negotiate? After all, at some point you realize that being bad is not so bad at all, it’s even more fun. And there are already too many superheroes in the world.
